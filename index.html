Millbrook Wildcats Basketball

Matt Kuppinen

I’m Matt Kuppinen, a Raleigh, NC point guard in the Class of 2028, and I play for Millbrook High School. At 5'9", I take pride in my shooting, pace, and decision-making, and I work to be a guard who can stretch the floor and compete every possession. In the 2025-26 season, I averaged 12.4 points per game, finished as the top three-point shooter in the CAP 8, and ranked first in all of 8A basketball in North Carolina in three-pointers made.
